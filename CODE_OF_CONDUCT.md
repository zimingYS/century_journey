# Code of Conduct
# 行为准则

## Our Pledge
## 我们的承诺

We want Century Journey to be a welcoming project for players, modders, artists, and developers. Please be respectful, constructive, and patient when participating in issues, pull requests, discussions, and community spaces.

我们希望《世纪之旅》能成为一个欢迎玩家、模组作者、美术创作者和开发者参与的项目。在参与 issue、Pull Request、讨论和社区空间时，请保持尊重、建设性和耐心。

## Expected Behavior
## 期望行为

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
  使用友善且包容的语言。
- Respect different experience levels and backgrounds.
  尊重不同的经验水平和背景。
- Give and receive feedback constructively.
  以建设性的方式提出和接受反馈。
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its community.
  让讨论聚焦于项目和社区本身。
- Credit other people's work where appropriate.
  在适当场景下标明他人的工作成果。

## Unacceptable Behavior
## 不可接受的行为

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
  骚扰、威胁、侮辱或人身攻击。
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
  歧视性语言或行为。
- Publishing private information without permission.
  未经许可公开他人隐私信息。
- Spam, trolling, or repeated off-topic disruption.
  垃圾信息、恶意扰乱或反复偏离主题。
- Misrepresenting ownership or licensing of code or assets.
  歪曲代码或资源的所有权与许可证信息。

## Enforcement
## 执行方式

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues or pull requests, and restrict participation when behavior harms the project or community.

当某些行为伤害项目或社区时，项目维护者可以删除评论、关闭 issue 或 Pull Request，并限制相关参与。

To report a concern, contact the maintainers through the repository. Reports will be handled with care and discretion.

如需报告问题，请通过仓库联系维护者。相关报告会被谨慎、妥善地处理。
